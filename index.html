Falling Tile Matching

How to Play

Objective: Score points by matching groups of 4 or more same-colored blocks!

Gameplay:

  • You'll see a pair of blocks (NOW pair) and the Next pair that will come after. The block with the 'Ã—' is the primary block.
  • To place blocks, tap (click) on an empty cell in the playfield. This will be the position of the primary block.
  • Drag your finger (mouse) away from the primary block (up, down, left, or right) to position the secondary block next to it.
    Release to place the pair.
  • The placed blocks will fall to the lowest available position in their columns.
  • When 4 or more blocks of the same color are touching (not diagonally), they will clear, and you'll earn points.
  • Clearing multiple groups with one placement increases your combo for more points!
  • Use the "Save" button to swap the current pair with a saved pair. If no pair is saved, the current pair will be stored.
  • The game is over when there are no valid empty spots left to place a pair of blocks.

Game Modes:

  • Relax Mode: Play at your own pace. No timers!
  • Timed Mode: Score as many points as you can before the 100-second timer runs out.
  • Pressure Mode: You have a limited time to make each move. This time gets shorter with each successful placement!
    If you run out of time, a pair will be placed for you at a random empty column.
